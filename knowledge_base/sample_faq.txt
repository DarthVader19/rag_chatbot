Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What are your shipping options?
A: We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days), expedited shipping (2-3 business days), and next-day shipping. Shipping costs vary based on the option selected and the delivery location.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can use this number to track your package's progress.

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition, unused, and with all tags attached. To initiate a return, please visit the returns section on our website or contact customer support.

Q: How do I get a refund for a returned item?
A: Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund. The refund will be credited to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times and costs vary. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees or import duties.

Q: My order was delayed. What should I do?
A: We apologize for the inconvenience. Please check the tracking information for the latest updates. If there has been no movement for several days or if the delivery is significantly late, please contact our customer support team with your order number, and we will investigate the issue.
